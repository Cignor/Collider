@002 What was the earliest source of music Africans found in the US which fed into the early Spirituals and Sorrow Songs?

\*Hymns composed, collected and published by Isaac Watts.
\Patriotic songs Dickinson and Boyce.

Their stark language and blunt metaphors of physical tribulation and spiritual transcendence spoke directly to the slaves experience.  Later Spirituals developed more elaborate imagery. <3
